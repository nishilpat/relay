How Relay works

Signal in. Follow-through out.

1

Customer submits a question

AE shares a Relay link after a call. Customer fills in their question, blocker, and timeline.

2

AE pastes post-call notes

Raw meeting notes or transcript paste directly into the Mac app. No formatting required.

3

Claude classifies and extracts

Intent, urgency, revenue impact, deal blockers, and stakeholders — structured from messy input.

4

Outputs are ready to send

Customer email, Salesforce note, Slack update, product request, and task list — one click to copy.

Trust & privacy

Customers choose what to share.

Relay never records screens or captures data automatically. Everything shared through the intake form is voluntary and explicit.

🔒

No automatic screen capture

Screenshots and files are optional and customer-initiated. Relay never takes screenshots automatically.

✋

Customer-controlled sharing

The intake form shows customers exactly what they're sharing before they submit. No hidden data collection.

🤝

Human confirmation before sending

Every generated output requires AE review and a deliberate copy or send action. Nothing is sent automatically.
